Welcome to the REYNOIRD lab!

Protein methylation dynamics in cancer



Our laboratory is interested in deciphering new lysine methylation signaling and their implication in cancer.

Lysine methylation is a covalent post-translational modification (PTM) contributing to all aspects of cell biology, and which deregulation often leads to human pathologies such as cancer.

Our group exists since 2017 and was officially created in 2023 as an emerging team from the Institute for Advanced Biosciences, in the great surroundings of Grenoble, FRANCE.

We are keen to consider applications in our laboratory for a student internship, as well as for a PhD or a postdoctoral project: please provide your CV and a motivation letter. Feel free to contact us for any inquiries!
